My Favorite Stuff

This is a site I've made for my class. It features stuff I like. I hope you enjoy!

Hello! My name is Christina. I'm studying Computer Science at UT Martin. I like programming, watching Netflix, and playing games.

I accidentally fell in love with computer science when, as an English major, I took an introductory CSCI course as an elective. I had so much fun that semester that I instantly changed my major. I love how creative coding can be, and how rewarding it is to finally solve that problem you've been stuck on. It's like a never-ending puzzle, and the horizons of the subject continue to expand every day!

I really like watching Netflix to unwind after a long day. My favorite shows are Black Mirror, Bojack Horseman, and Stranger Things. I also enjoy documentaries. I especially like documentaries about different cultures and ways of living. Of course, I also enjoy silly stuff like reality shows and cheesy horror and romance movies. My husband and I look forward to the onslaught of terrible Halloween and Christmas movies every year.

Without a doubt, I wouldn't be the person I am today without gaming. I love both video and tabletop games. I have fond memories of inching through Half-Life as a child, wracked with fear of what horror lurked around the next corner. I remember my first Level 100 Pokemon (Vaporeon), and the thrill of finally beating my brother in Smash. Equally fond are my memories of tabletop games. My family was never big on watching TV or going on trips together, but we all love playing board games. UNO was the big thing growing up. My grandma loved to sneak 6's instead of 9's and vice versa. Today, I collect board games and have a fairly nice collection. I also enjoy Dungeons and Dragons, and act as DM for an ongoing campaign.
